Restaurants in downtown

There are many restaurants near campus on 9th Street in downtown Columbia:

Kinkao (Thai), Bamboo (Chinese), Hokkaido Ramen (Japanese), Cafe Poland (Polish), 44 Canteen (American), Flat Branch (Pub & Brewery), Uprise Bakery (Bakery & Coffee), Chipotle (Mexican), Bangkok Garden (Thai), Addison's American Grill (American).

Walk times from campus to downtown 9th Street are about 15–20 minutes.
